About Yordy Turkey Farm

For generations, the Yordy family has raised high-quality turkeys in the heart of Central Illinois. Located in Morton, IL – known as the Turkey Capital of the World – we take pride in providing fresh, locally raised turkeys to families across the region.

Every bird is raised on our farm with the space and care needed to grow naturally. We believe in sustainable, responsible farming that results in better-tasting turkeys and a healthier environment.
